## ðŸŽ›ï¸ Main Controls

The main interface has 6 essential controls:

### CUTOFF
- **What it does**: Controls the filter frequency (brightness)
- **Turn left**: Darker, more muffled sound
- **Turn right**: Brighter, more open sound
- **Sweet spot**: Around 500Hz - 2kHz for bass

### RESONANCE
- **What it does**: Adds emphasis at the cutoff frequency
- **Turn left**: Smooth, natural sound
- **Turn right**: More character and edge
- **Sweet spot**: 20-40% for subtle emphasis

### DRIVE
- **What it does**: Adds warmth and saturation
- **Turn left**: Clean sound
- **Turn right**: Gritty, distorted sound
- **Sweet spot**: 30-50% for warmth without distortion

### SUB MIX
- **What it does**: Blends in a pure sub-bass oscillator
- **Turn left**: No sub bass
- **Turn right**: Maximum sub bass
- **Sweet spot**: 40-60% for powerful low end

### THICC
- **What it does**: Makes the sound wider and thicker (unison detune)
- **Turn left**: Mono, focused sound
- **Turn right**: Wide, thick sound
- **Sweet spot**: 30-50% for width without losing focus

### GLIDE
- **What it does**: Smooth pitch slide between notes
- **Turn left**: No glide (instant pitch changes)
- **Turn right**: Slow, smooth glide
- **Sweet spot**: 100-300ms for expressive basslines

## ðŸŽµ Making Your First Bass Sound

### Classic Sub Bass
1. Load preset: **"Deep Sub"**
2. Turn **SUB MIX** to 60%
3. Turn **CUTOFF** to around 400Hz
4. Turn **RESONANCE** to 30%
5. Add **DRIVE** to taste (30-40%)

### Wobble Bass
1. Load preset: **"Wobble Bass"**
2. Click **ADVANCED**
3. Adjust **LFO RATE** for wobble speed
4. Adjust **LFO AMOUNT** for wobble depth
5. Fine-tune **CUTOFF** and **RESONANCE**

### Mono Bass (For Mixing)
1. Turn **THICC** to 0%
2. Set **VOICES** to 1 (in Advanced panel)
3. Adjust **SUB MIX** for low-end power

### Wide Synth Bass
1. Turn **THICC** to 50-70%
2. Set **VOICES** to 3-5 (in Advanced panel)
3. Turn **CUTOFF** higher (1-3kHz)
4. Add **DRIVE** for character
